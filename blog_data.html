Power of Data Analytics

Data analytics is the science of analyzing raw data to uncover trends and insights that inform decision-making. It plays a vital role in business, healthcare, and technology.

Using tools like Python, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI, analysts can visualize patterns, predict outcomes, and optimize processes.

The ability to translate data into actionable insights gives organizations a competitive edge in today’s data-driven world.
